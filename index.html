About Me

Passionate about creating positive relationships between dogs and their families

ðŸ‘‹ Meet Your Trainer

Hi, I'm Anna! I didn't start in the dog world. I spent years building a career in a completely different field: structured, demanding, and far from anything to do with dogs. I had degrees, responsibilities, and a clear path laid out in front of me. But deep down, I felt disconnected, like something important was missing.

Ace shook everything I thought I knew. I tried every method I could find: treats, tools, techniques, but nothing worked. I felt like I was failing him completely. Until one day, I stopped following everyone else's advice and started listening to him and to myself. That moment changed everything. I discovered the truth: it's not about controlling dogs. It's about understanding them. And more importantly, understanding ourselves.

That's how Pawpose was born. This is more than dog training; it's a fresh approach to how we live with and lead our dogs. I'm here to help people build genuine relationships with their dogs, free from gimmicks and rooted in trust.

I left my old career behind for this work. Because this isn't just something I do, it's who I am now.

"Every dog has the potential to be amazing. My job is to help you unlock that potential together."

Training in Action

See Anna's training methods and real results with dogs

Perfect Heel & Focus with Oscar

Oscar waits patiently at the roadside, follows Anna's lead with perfect timing, and heels on command while maintaining full attention without distractions like sniffing.

Immediate Recall with Callie

Callie walks attentively beside Anna, checking in regularly. When distracted by sniffing, she immediately responds to the heel command, showing excellent focus.

Owner Interaction with Bobby

Bobby responds to hand signals for sit, then shows incredible recall by leaving a ball mid-chase to return to his owner and lie down awaiting the next command.
